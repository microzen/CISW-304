Eggs Benedict

Fried chicken wings coated in cayenne pepper hot sauce and dipped in blue cheese: Who doesn't love that buffalo stuff? The now ubiquitous bar snack was first invented in 1964 at the Anchor Bar in Buffalo, New York, though there are other disputing claims. In 1977, the city of Buffalo declared July 29 to be the official Chicken Wing Day. 12.99$
